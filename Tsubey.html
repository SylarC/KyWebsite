The One and Only Tsubey Pasta

Ingredients:

Sausage, Bacon, Bell Pepper, Mushrooms, Garlic, Kechup, Butter, Salt, Sphagetti, and Vegetable Oil

How to make it:

(1) Cut the sausage obliquely to 5 mm width. Cut the onions into 7 mm wide combs and loosen. Cut the peppers in half in half and remove the seeds, cut into 7 mm length.
(2) Boil 3 liters of hot water in a pan, add 2 tablespoons of coarse salt and start spaghetti in soup according to the display time.
(3) Try 1 tablespoonful of oil 1/1/2, onion, garlic in a frying pan and fry it with a medium fiery medium. When the onion is clear, add sausages, mushy mushrooms and green peppers in order, and stir fry each time.
(4) Add tomato ketchup, salt and pepper, stir fry 3-4 minutes to spread it while spreading. When the moisture of ketchup flies and becomes familiar with vegetables and the color becomes dark, add 1/2 cup of boiled juice from the pot of (2) and mix the whole.
(5) In addition to boiling spaghetti water (leave a little boiled juice) (4), add butter and fry together. When moisture is not enough, adjust with boiled juice.
